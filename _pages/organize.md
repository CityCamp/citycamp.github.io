Get started organizing a CityCamp where you live:

1. Review the [CityCamp playbook](/playbook).
1. [Review the ACT code of conduct](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1g5CBM9ccfElqQPeaTh0OQGyzbQH-NE8T8TemuL2jz94/edit?tab=t.0).
1. [Check status](/events) (someone may have started organizing one in your city).
1. Join the [ACT Discord](https://discord.gg/EM6ywtMhkP) and choose "CityCamp" for the question "Are you part of an ACT Connection?"
1. Secure a location and set a date.
1. [Add your event](/events/new) to the calendar.
